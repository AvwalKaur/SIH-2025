SimCity: Disaster Manager

How to Play

  • Phase 1 - Planning: Build resilient city infrastructure with hospitals, fire stations, and evacuation routes
  • Phase 2 - Response: Manage real-time disaster response by deploying emergency units and controlling traffic
  • Phase 3 - Rebuilding: Assess damage, repair buildings, and upgrade infrastructure for future resilience
  • Run practice drills to test your city's emergency preparedness
  • Balance budget between development, safety measures, and post-disaster recovery
  • Maintain citizen morale through community services and effective disaster management

Strategy Tips

  • Build emergency services early - they provide both coverage and emergency units
  • Create multiple evacuation routes to prevent traffic jams during disasters
  • Practice drills improve your disaster response effectiveness
  • Upgrade damaged buildings to disaster-resistant versions for long-term resilience
  • Community centers boost morale and help attract new residents
  • Take loans only when necessary - interest payments reduce long-term funds
