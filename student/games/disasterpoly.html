Disaster Poly

How to Play

  • Roll dice to move around the emergency preparedness board
  • Buy Properties to collect rent from other players
  • Complete Property Groups to double rent and build facilities
  • Draw Community Chest and Chance cards for events
  • Avoid going to the Emergency Shelter (jail)
  • Build your emergency preparedness empire and bankrupt opponents!

Space Types

  • Properties - Buyable emergency facilities (8 color groups)
  • Evacuation Routes - Emergency transportation (4 railroads)
  • Utilities - Essential services (2 utilities)
  • Community Chest - Community emergency actions
  • Chance - Unexpected disaster scenarios
  • Tax Spaces - Emergency preparedness taxes

Monopoly Rules

  • Property Groups: Own all properties in a color group to double rent
  • Evacuation Routes: Rent increases with number owned (1-4 routes)
  • Utilities: Rent = dice roll × 4 (1 owned) or × 10 (both owned)
  • Emergency Shelter: 3 ways out - roll doubles, pay $50, or use special card
  • Free Parking: Collect all tax money accumulated
  • Doubles: Roll again! But 3 doubles in a row = go to jail

Disaster Events Include:

  • Tornado Warning
  • Flood Alert
  • Wildfire
  • Power Outage
  • Heat Wave
  • Blizzard
  • Hurricane
  • Severe Storm
