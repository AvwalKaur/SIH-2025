Disaster Poly

How to Play

  • Roll dice to move around the disaster preparedness board
  • Buy Emergency Services to protect your community
  • Avoid Disaster Zones or pay the penalty
  • Collect resources and improve your preparedness
  • Draw disaster cards and respond to emergencies
  • First to reach 1000 preparedness points wins!

Space Types

  • Emergency Services - Buyable protective facilities
  • Disaster Zones - Pay penalty or use resources
  • Safe Zones - Gain resources and safety
  • Special Events - Random disaster scenarios
  • Corner Spaces - Major emergency events

Resources

  • Money: Buy emergency services and supplies
  • Preparedness: Your disaster readiness score
  • Supplies: Emergency kits and equipment
  • Knowledge: Training and education points

Disaster Events Include:

  • Tornado Warning
  • Flood Alert
  • Wildfire
  • Power Outage
  • Heat Wave
  • Blizzard
  • Hurricane
  • Severe Storm
